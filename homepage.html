About Us

Welcome to Massavu Ivestment Club, where financial aspiration meets strategic investments. Our mission is to empower individuals through colaborative investing, education and comunity engagement.

Membership

To become a member of the Massavu Investment Club, individuals are required to participate in monthly savings until the end of the year. The club functions as an investment pool, where members contribute a set amount of money on a regular basis, and these funds are then managed collectively to pursue investment opportunities. The club aims to provide a platform for members to pool resources and invest collectively, potentially accessing investment opportunities that might not be available to individual members.

To sign up for membership, you would typically need to adhere to the following steps:

Membership Application: Complete a membership application form, providing personal details and agreeing to the club's terms and conditions.

Contribution Commitment: Commit to making regular monthly contributions to the club, with a specified minimum amount.

Membership Approval: Await approval from the club's administrators after submitting the application and initial contribution.

Participation: Once approved, actively participate in the club's activities, attend meetings, and engage in the investment decision-making process with other members.

It's important to note that the specific requirements, terms, and conditions for joining the Massavu Investment Club may vary and should be obtained directly from the club's administrators or through their official communication channels.
